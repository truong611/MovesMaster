Introducing moves matter
For many of us, movement is something we take for granted.
Whether we are in the great outdoors, commuting to school or work, or simply pottering in the garden we might be counting steps with a fitness device, but we aren’t thinking about how we can make our steps count..
Welcome to a world where moves matter At Moves Matter, it is our dream to enable businesses and charities to come together to provide charitable giving through the simple act of movement. And that means ANY kind of movement. We believe that if people want to give to charity they should not be held back by disability or impairment.
Our values
IMPACTFUL We want our work and concept to have an impact, not only on the problems of the world, but on those who believe they cannot make a difference. We’d like to support and be part of a global change of consciousness.

POSITIVITY Moves Matter has confidence in giving cause for hope by bridging the gap between big companies and individuals, through education, awareness and global connectivity.

GENEROSITY Generosity plays a big part within our brand. We want to encourage companies and people to be liberal with giving and sharing and knowing that even a small amount done collectively makes a big difference.
