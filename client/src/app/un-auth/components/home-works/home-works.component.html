Why moves matter for charities
Charities continually seek publicity and funding for their causes and to meet their charitable aims.
Charities will be invited to register on the Moves Matter platform where their details will be searchable to companies and the public. The means to highlight their latest appeals will be available and a platform-wide News-feed will keep their presence in the user consciousness.
Charities seek funding from companies who earmark considerable donation budgets for both practical and altruistic reasons.
Why moves matter for companies
It’s no secret that companies exist to make money. However, increasingly in today’s world, to attract customers, employees and shareholders, they also need to prove that they are giving back to society at large.
More and more companies are therefore subscribing to, and leveraging, their social responsibility activity to help illustrate their credentials and differentiate themselves from the competition.
Bringing charities and companies together
Moves Matter brings charities and companies together to generate charitable giving through the simple act of movement.
Moves Matter provides the means for charities and companies to develop fund-raising campaigns, with an agreed, nominated financial target, and share them with the public.
While ‘live’ other companies can choose to ‘match’ the campaign, committing to pay the equivalent financial contribution to the charity.
Effectively, the charity’s causes and the company’s altruism is made public in a social media setting.
Why moves matter for the public
The Moves Matter platform is not another passive, scrolling social media setting. Instead, Moves Matter App users will be engaged and motivated, using their movement to contribute to causes on an ongoing basis, moving with purpose, giving back to society.
Moves Matter provides the means for charities and companies to develop fund-raising campaigns, with an agreed, nominated financial target, and share them with the public.
The Moves Matter App will upload the user’s activity and convert it to ‘Moves’ - a currency - which can be applied across one or more of the user’s favoured campaigns. Once the Moves donated reaches the campaign’s target, the campaign is fully funded and the company pays the agreed amount to the charity.
The app will also enable users to make financial donations to charities, share their giving across other social media platforms and even lodge requests inviting their favourite charities to join Moves Matter.
